Untraditional Path

    About Me

  • As a child, I was always building something from scratch, whether they were automated toy cars or a computer. I was built and educated to be a doctor or an engineer.
  • In college, I fell in love with Journalism and Law. Enjoyed it, and it taught me different types of communication and storytelling.
  • Graduating shortly, I ended up jumping into business, and learned the micro-and macro ins and outs of decision making from top to bottom.
  • Discovered three years into my business career, the job of designers and how it is a mixture of science, creativity, and logic. I never felt more understood in the way I think.
  • Left my business job and went backpacking in Africa.
  • Found a job at an engineering firm and put myself back to school Took a risk, rather, an investment in myself, to put myself back to school and get the skills I needed to become a designer.

  • So here I am -

    a designer with transferable skills from different corners of my career,
    freshly out of UI/UX curriculum at the University of Denver.
